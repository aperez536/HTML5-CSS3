General information

The northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing in Chicago's northside neighboorhoods on environment in witch to learn and pkay soccer. To be a menber of NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4-12 and reside in a Chicago northside neighborhood. NYSL is ran by a small full-time staff, and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league menbers.
